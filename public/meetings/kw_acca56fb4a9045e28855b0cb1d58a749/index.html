The Kronenwetter Village Board voted 6-0 to terminate the employment of the Community Development Director for just cause at a special meeting called April 30, 2026. Discussion

Closed Session

Trustee Coyle moved and Trustee Mortensen seconded a motion to convene into closed session under Wis. Stat. s. 19.85(1)(c) and 19.85(1)(f) to review a specific personnel matter and address next steps. The motion carried 7-0 on a roll call vote. Closed session began at 4:32 p.m. Trustee Sorensen left the meeting around 6:00 p.m. during the closed session.

Termination of Community Development Director

Upon reconvening in open session, Trustee Lesniak moved and Trustee Myszka seconded a motion to terminate the employment of the Community Development Director for just cause. The motion carried 6-0 on a roll call vote. Village President Joling stated that additional personnel issues would be addressed by Village Administrator Davel, that open records notices would be handled by legal staff, and that he asked board members to sign the letter of termination.

Action items

  • Community Development Director terminated for just cause, effective April 30, 2026, by a 6-0 board vote.
  • Village Administrator Davel directed to address remaining personnel issues arising from the matter.
  • Legal staff directed to handle open records notices related to the termination.
  • Village Board members asked by President Joling to sign the letter of termination.
