Public Hearing re: Waiver of Minutes for Riverside Elementary

The board was scheduled to hold a public hearing as required under Wisconsin Statute 118.38(1)(b) regarding a waiver of instructional minutes for Riverside Elementary. Supporting materials included a Notice of Public Hearing and waiver application documents.

Soaring into Summer

Presenters Kelly Ziegelbauer (Weston Principal), Lauren Doescher, and Katie Wochinski were scheduled to present information regarding a 'Soaring into Summer' initiative, with supporting presentation materials provided.

Annual Required Student Data Reports

Dr. Lindell and Gina Lehman were scheduled to present annual required student data reports covering homeless and foster care status, seclusion and restraint incidents, crime statistics on school property, nursing reports, at-risk students nearing graduation, continuum of services, and emergency support services. The presentation was expected to include multiple supporting documents detailing student services and student support outcomes.

Education for Employment/ACP Report

Aaron Hoffman and student Takoda Rowland were scheduled to present a report on the Education for Employment and Alternative Career Pathway programs for the district.

Resolution for Riverside Elementary Waiver of Minutes

Dr. Strike was scheduled to present a resolution regarding the waiver of instructional hours for Riverside Elementary, with supporting documents including the waiver application and resolution language for board consideration.

Junior High and Idea Out-of-State Trips

Dr. Lindell was scheduled to present three out-of-state trip requests: a junior high trip to Washington, D.C. for 2026-27, a junior high world language trip, and an Idea senior class trip, with supporting documentation for each proposal.

Staff Compensation Plan

Dr. Strike was scheduled to present the 2026-2027 staff compensation plans for board consideration, with supporting materials outlining compensation proposals for certified staff.

2026-2027 Budget Update

Dr. Strike was scheduled to provide a comprehensive update on the 2026-2027 budget, including preliminary budget presentation materials for board review and discussion.

Year-End Spending 2025-2026

Dr. Strike was scheduled to present a year-end spending report for the 2025-2026 fiscal year, providing financial details on district expenditures as the school year concludes.

2026-2027 Budgetary Spending Approval

Dr. Strike was scheduled to request board approval for 2026-2027 budgetary spending authority, with supporting documentation outlining the proposed spending framework.

First Reading of Policies

Dr. Nye, Dr. Lindell, and Dr. Strike were scheduled to present first readings of seven policies including Definitions (po0100), Public Comment at Board Meetings (po0167.3), Employment of Professional Staff (po3120), Employment of Personnel in Community Education Programs (po3120.05), Non-Renewal, Resignation, and Termination (po3140), Employment of Substitutes (po4120.04), and Full-Time Student (po5200.01), with a memo regarding credit limits for senior high students.
