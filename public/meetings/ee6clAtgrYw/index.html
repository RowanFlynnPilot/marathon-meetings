Discussion

Election of Officers

Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All elections passed unanimously without opposition.

Minutes of Previous Meeting

The minutes from April 21st were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest

Brandon Jill from 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported last year's attendance was estimated at 11,000-12,000 people and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was cancelled due to permit issues. A commissioner noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year and requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future decisions. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request for improved hotel usage data in their post-event report. Motion passed unanimously.

Remarks from Commission Members

Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, where she met the candidates and the winner Anastasia. She noted the candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese and were impressed with the community. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.
