## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for Chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as Secretary.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from April 21st were approved with a motion and second by Renee. Passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members questioned hotel room usage, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room data for future applications. A motion was made to approve $5,000 (same as last year), seconded by Jackson, and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting winner Anastasia had three bachelor's degrees. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s. The candidates visited local attractions including Mullen's Cheese and expressed appreciation for the Marathon County community.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
