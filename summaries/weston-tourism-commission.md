## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also heard that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield has been canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated for secretary and approved unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from April 21st were approved unanimously. The motion was seconded by Renee.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year. He announced that the competing Marshfield hot air balloon event has been canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members questioned room tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request for better hotel room data in the post-event report. Motion passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, noting the candidates were impressed with Marathon County. The winner, Anastasia, sat at their table. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
