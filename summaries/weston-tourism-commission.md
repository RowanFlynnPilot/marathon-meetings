## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair, seconded by Jackson, and elected unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary unanimously. There was brief procedural confusion about whether the Vice Chair nomination had been properly seconded.

### Minutes of the Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously with no discussion.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making them the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commissioner Hodal asked about room tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future decisions. A motion to approve $5,000 (same as last year) was made and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, noting the candidates were impressed with Marathon County. She mentioned meeting Anastasia, who won the title. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
