## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated by a commissioner and seconded by Jackson, then elected Vice Chair unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected Secretary unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented the grant request. He reported last year's attendance was estimated at 11,000-12,000 and announced that the competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. A commissioner questioned room usage, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission discussed the importance of tracking hotel room data for future decisions. The commission approved a $5,000 grant (same as last year) unanimously, with a request for better hotel room tracking data in the post-event report.

### Commission Member Remarks - Alice in Dairyland Event
Renee reported attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She noted the candidates were from various Wisconsin universities, and Anastasia won the competition. The candidates toured Marathon County attractions including Mullen's Cheese. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.
