## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission noted that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as secretary.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from April 21st were approved unanimously with a motion and second by Renee.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) represented the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year. He announced that the competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year and requested better tracking of hotel room usage from the Holiday Inn Express and Fairfield. The commission approved a $5,000 grant unanimously, matching last year's amount, with a request for detailed room usage data in their post-event report.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting winner Anastasia had three bachelor's degrees. The candidates visited Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese, and this was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.

No public comment was offered.
