## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for Chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as Secretary.

### Approval of Previous Meeting Minutes
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved unanimously. The motion was seconded by Renee.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane represented the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. A commissioner questioned room usage, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room data for future applications. A motion to approve $5,000 (matching last year's grant) was made and passed unanimously, with Jackson seconding.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, describing the venue and meeting the candidates. She noted the winner, Anastasia, sat at their table. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s. The candidates visited local attractions including Mullen's Cheese and were impressed with the community.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
