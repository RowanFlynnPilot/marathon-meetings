## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant request for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also heard that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield has been canceled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for Chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by a commissioner, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as Secretary.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill from 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented the grant request. He reported estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event has been canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. A commissioner questioned room usage, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. Last year's grant was $5,000. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future applications. Motion to approve $5,000 grant was made, seconded by Jackson, and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, describing it as a neat venue. She noted the candidates were from various Wisconsin colleges, and Anastasia won the competition. This was the first time the event had been hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s. Renee said the candidates were thankful for the community hospitality.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
