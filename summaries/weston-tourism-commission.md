## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also received a report on the Alice in Dairyland event recently hosted in Marathon County.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected officers for the reorganized commission. Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as secretary.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was made and seconded by Renee, passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) presented the grant request for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year and noted a competing Marshfield event has been canceled due to permit issues, making them the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members questioned room night tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission requested better room tracking data from hotels for future applications. Motion to approve $5,000 grant (same as last year) was made by Erling, seconded by Jackson, and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She noted the candidates visited various Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese, and that Anastasia won the competition. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
