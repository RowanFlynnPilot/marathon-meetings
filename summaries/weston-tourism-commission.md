## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and elected unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by a member, seconded by Jackson, and elected unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as secretary unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) represented the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making their event the sole balloon festival in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year and requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future applications. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a motion passed unanimously. Commission requested the applicant provide hotel room data in their post-event report.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, noting it was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County in decades. She described meeting the candidates, including winner Anastasia, and noted the candidates toured various Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
