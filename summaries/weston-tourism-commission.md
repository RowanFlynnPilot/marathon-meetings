## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also received an update that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially boosting attendance for the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and unanimously elected as Chair. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by Jackson and elected unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All elections passed without opposition.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from April 21st were approved. Motion was made and seconded by Renee. Passed unanimously without discussion.

### Grant Request: Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the event, reporting 11,000-12,000 attendees last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year, and requested better tracking of hotel room usage from the Holiday Inn Express. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request for detailed room data in the post-event report. Motion passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, noting the candidates were impressed with Marathon County. She mentioned meeting Anastasia, who won the competition, and described how candidates visited local attractions including Mullen's Cheese. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s.
