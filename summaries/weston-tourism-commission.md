## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated and elected as Vice Chair with Jackson providing the second. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All elections passed unanimously with no opposition.

### Minutes of previous meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant request: Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill from 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented the grant request. He reported approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commissioner Hodal questioned room tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year when they received $5,000. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with Jackson seconding. Motion passed unanimously. The commission requested better hotel room tracking data for future reports.

### Remarks from commission members
Renee reported attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar with Jamie. She met the candidates and noted that Anastasia, who sat at their table, won the title. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s. The candidates were from various Wisconsin universities and were impressed with Marathon County's community.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
