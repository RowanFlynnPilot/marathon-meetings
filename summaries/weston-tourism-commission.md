## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission noted that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by Hodal, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting approximately 11,000-12,000 total attendance last year. He announced that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled due to permit issues, making their event the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. A commissioner requested better tracking of hotel room usage to help with future grant decisions. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with motion passing unanimously. The applicant was asked to provide hotel room data and report on whether the Marshfield cancellation affected attendance.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She noted the contestants were from various Wisconsin universities and were impressed with Marathon County attractions including Mullen's Cheese. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s. The winner, Anastasia, sat at their table.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
