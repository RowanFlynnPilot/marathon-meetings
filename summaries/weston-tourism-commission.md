## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also heard that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected Trustee Erling as Chair, Cushing as Vice Chair (seconded by Jackson), and Renee as Secretary. All elections passed unanimously with no opposition.

### Approval of Minutes
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) presented the grant request, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year and requested better tracking of hotel room usage from Holiday Inn Express. The commission approved a $5,000 grant, matching last year's amount, with a request for improved attendance and room data in the post-event report. Motion passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, describing how the candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese. She noted this was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s, and the candidates were thankful for the community's hospitality.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
