## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and elected unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as secretary unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) represented the event, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year despite last year's $5,000 grant. A commissioner requested better tracking of hotel room usage from the hotels directly. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request that the organizers provide hotel usage data in their post-event report. Motion passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, describing it as a unique venue. She noted meeting the candidates, including Anastasia who won the title. The candidates visited Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese, marking the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s. The candidates expressed appreciation for the community.
