## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also heard that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected three officers unanimously. Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair. Cushing was nominated by a commissioner, seconded by Jackson, and elected as Vice Chair. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All votes passed without opposition.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) represented the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that the competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. A commissioner noted the application showed 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year and requested better tracking of hotel room usage from the Holiday Inn Express where pilots stay. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request for better room tracking data in their post-event report. Motion passed unanimously, seconded by Jackson.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting winner Anastasia had three bachelor's degrees. The candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s. Renee noted the candidates were appreciative of the community.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
