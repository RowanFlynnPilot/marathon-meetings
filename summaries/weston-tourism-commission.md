## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by a member, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated for secretary, seconded, and approved unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from April 21st were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commissioner Hodal questioned room tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request that organizers provide better hotel room data in their post-event report. Motion passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting winner Anastasia had three bachelor's degrees. The candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
