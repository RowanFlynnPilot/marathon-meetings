## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also heard that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield has been canceled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated by a commissioner and elected as Vice Chair unanimously, with Jackson providing the second. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. The motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill from 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year and announced that the competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole balloon event in the area. A commissioner noted they requested $5,000 but are booking 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year. The commission discussed the need for better room tracking data from hotels. The commission approved a $5,000 grant (same as last year) unanimously, with a request that the organization provide actual hotel room usage data in their post-event report.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting that Anastasia won the competition. The candidates toured Marathon County attractions including Mullen's Cheese. This was noted as the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
