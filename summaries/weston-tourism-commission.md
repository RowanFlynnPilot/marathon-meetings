## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission noted that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated and elected as Vice Chair with Jackson providing the second. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All elections passed unanimously by voice vote.

### Approval of Minutes
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously by voice vote.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) presented on behalf of the event, reporting approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year. He announced that the competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year while requesting the same funding. A commissioner requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future applications. The commission approved a $5,000 grant (same as last year), with Jackson seconding the motion. Passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, including winner Anastasia, and noted the contestants were impressed with Marathon County hospitality. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
