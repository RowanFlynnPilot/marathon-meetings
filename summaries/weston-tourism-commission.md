## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was cancelled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected officers for the reorganized committee. Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated and elected as Vice Chair with Jackson providing the second. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All elections passed unanimously with no opposition.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. The motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported last year's attendance was estimated at 11,000-12,000 people and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was cancelled due to permit issues, making their event the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year and requested better tracking of hotel usage for future reports. The commission approved a $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with the motion passing unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting the winner Anastasia had three bachelor's degrees. The candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese and expressed gratitude for the community's hospitality. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
