## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as secretary.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from April 21st were approved unanimously, seconded by Renee.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill (3308 Green Pastures Lane) represented the event, reporting estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year. He announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer hotel rooms than last year and requested better tracking of room usage from hotels. A commissioner requested room data be included in future reports. The commission approved a $5,000 grant (same as last year) unanimously, with plans to evaluate the impact of no Marshfield competition for next year's consideration.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates and noted that Anastasia won the competition. The candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese, and this was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
