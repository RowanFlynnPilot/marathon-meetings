## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected new officers for the reorganized commission. Trustee Erling was nominated and elected as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated by a commissioner and seconded by Jackson, then elected as Vice Chair unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary unanimously.

### Approval of Minutes
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was made and seconded by Renee. Passed unanimously with no discussion.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented the grant request. He reported estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year and announced that the competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole event in the area. A commissioner questioned room night tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year when they received $5,000. The commission discussed needing better hotel room data for future decisions. Motion was made to approve $5,000 grant matching last year's amount, with a request for post-event reporting on attendance changes. Passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates who toured Marathon County attractions including Mullen's Cheese. She noted this was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s. The winner, Anastasia, sat at their table and held three bachelor's degrees.

No public comment was offered.
