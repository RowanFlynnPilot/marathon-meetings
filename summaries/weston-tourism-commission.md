## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant to the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected Trustee Erling as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by a commissioner, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary unanimously.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
The minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest, reporting approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year. He announced that the competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making their event the sole hot air balloon event in the area. A commissioner noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room usage from the Holiday Inn Express for future reporting. A motion to approve $5,000 (same as last year) was made, seconded by Jackson, and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, describing meeting the candidates including winner Anastasia. She noted the candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese, and this was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
