## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated and elected Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair, seconded by Jackson, and elected unanimously. Renee was nominated and elected Secretary unanimously.

### Minutes of the previous meeting
Minutes from April 21st were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant request: Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented the request. He reported 11,000-12,000 total attendance last year and announced that the competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole event in the area. Commission members questioned room usage, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room data for future applications. A motion to approve $5,000 (same as last year) was made, seconded by Jackson, and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from commission members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates and noted that Anastasia won the competition. This was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since approximately the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
