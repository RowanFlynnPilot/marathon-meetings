## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission also heard that a competing balloon event in Marshfield has been canceled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected officers for the reorganized committee. Trustee Erling was nominated for Chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for Vice Chair by motion, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as Secretary.

### Approval of Minutes
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion carried with a second by Renee, passing unanimously.

### Grant Request: Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported estimated attendance of 11,000-12,000 last year and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event has been canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members questioned room night tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year. The commission approved a $5,000 grant (same as last year), with a request that organizers provide better hotel room data in their post-event report. Motion passed unanimously with Jackson seconding.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar, describing how the candidates toured Marathon County attractions including Mullen's Cheese. She noted the winner was Anastasia, and that this was the first time the event was hosted in Marathon County since the 1970s. The candidates expressed appreciation for the community.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
