## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its reorganization meeting, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was canceled, potentially increasing attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
The commission elected Trustee Erling as Chair unanimously. Cushing was nominated and elected as Vice Chair with Jackson providing the second. Renee was nominated and elected as Secretary. All elections passed unanimously by voice vote.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee. Passed unanimously by voice vote.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill of 3308 Green Pastures Lane presented on behalf of the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. He reported approximately 11,000-12,000 attendees last year and announced that a competing Marshfield balloon event was canceled due to permit issues, making theirs the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commission members noted the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year despite the same $5,000 request as 2024. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future reports. Motion to approve $5,000 grant was made by the Chair, seconded by Jackson, and passed unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, noting winner Anastasia was at her table. The candidates toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese and were impressed with the community. This was noted as the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
