## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Tourism Commission held its first meeting after reorganization, electing new officers and approving a $5,000 grant for the Taste and Glow Balloonfest. The commission learned that a competing hot air balloon event in Marshfield was cancelled, potentially boosting attendance at the Weston event.

## Key Discussions
### Election of Officers
Trustee Erling was nominated for chair and approved unanimously. Cushing was nominated for vice chair by a commissioner, seconded by Jackson, and approved unanimously. Renee was nominated and approved unanimously as secretary.

### Minutes of Previous Meeting
Minutes from the April 21st meeting were approved. Motion was seconded by Renee and passed unanimously.

### Grant Request - Taste and Glow Balloonfest
Brandon Jill from Taste and Glow Balloonfest (3308 Green Pastures Lane) presented their grant request. He reported 11,000-12,000 estimated attendees last year and announced that a competing Marshfield hot air balloon event was cancelled due to permit issues, making Taste and Glow the sole hot air balloon event in the area. Commissioner Hodal asked about room tracking, noting the application showed 20 fewer rooms than last year when they received $5,000. The commission requested better tracking of hotel room usage for future applications. Motion to approve $5,000 grant was made and seconded by Jackson, passing unanimously.

### Remarks from Commission Members
Renee reported on attending the Alice in Dairyland dinner at Red Rock Orchard in Edgar. She described meeting the candidates, including winner Anastasia, and noted the contestants toured Marathon County locations including Mullen's Cheese. This was the first time Marathon County hosted the event since the 1970s. The candidates expressed appreciation for the community.

## Public Comment
No public comment was offered.
