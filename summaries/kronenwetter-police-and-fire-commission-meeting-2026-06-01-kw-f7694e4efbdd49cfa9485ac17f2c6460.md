## Meeting Overview
The Village of Kronenwetter Police and Fire Commission voted 3-0 to reject a fire department applicant from continuing in the firefighter hiring process following a closed-session interview. The commission also elected new leadership, naming Commissioner Alex Vedvik as President and Commissioner Rick Smith as Vice-President. Approval of the April 27 minutes was tabled due to insufficient quorum of members who attended that prior meeting.

## Key Discussions
### Approval of Minutes from 04/27/2026
Commissioner Smith moved to table approval of the April 27, 2026 minutes until a future meeting at which a quorum of members who were also present at that prior meeting could act on them. Commissioner Vedvik seconded. The motion carried 3-0 by roll call vote.

### Election of Police and Fire Commission President and Vice-President
Commissioner Smith nominated Commissioner Alex Vedvik for President; no other nominations were received and Vedvik accepted. Commissioner Rule seconded. The motion carried with Rule and Smith voting aye and Vedvik abstaining. Newly elected President Vedvik then nominated Commissioner Rick Smith for Vice-President; no other nominations were received and Smith accepted. Commissioner Rule seconded. The motion carried with Rule and Vedvik voting aye and Smith abstaining.

### Closed Session - Fire Department Applicant Interview
Vice-President Smith moved, seconded by Commissioner Rule, to convene into closed session pursuant to Wis. Stat. 19.85(1)(c) to interview a fire department applicant. The motion carried 3-0 by roll call vote and the commission entered closed session at 5:09 p.m. with Chief Theresa O'Brien invited to participate. The commission reconvened in open session at 5:47 p.m. on a motion by Vice-President Smith, seconded by President Vedvik, which carried 3-0.

### Action After Closed Session
Following the closed-session interview of a fire department applicant, Vice-President Smith moved to not continue the applicant in the firefighter hiring process. Commissioner Rule seconded. The motion carried 3-0 by roll call vote, effectively ending that applicant's candidacy.

## Public Comment
No public comment was recorded.
