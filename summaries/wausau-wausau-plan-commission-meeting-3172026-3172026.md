## Meeting Overview
The Wausau Plan Commission approved two key items: a conditional use permit for a 70-unit, 7-story apartment building at 731 North First Street for Beacon Resources LLC, and a transportation project plat for Grand Avenue signal replacements. A public hearing was held regarding a proposed personal storage facility at 218 South Fourth Street, with the applicants speaking in support.

## Key Discussions
### Minutes from February 18th
Bugamman made a motion to approve the minutes, seconded by Balkan. The motion passed unanimously with all voting in favor.

### Public hearing on conditional use permit for 218 South Fourth Street (Done Storage)
Jason Dunwy and Melinda Dunwy spoke in favor of the storage facility proposal. They argued that with over 400 new apartment units recently approved downtown, including the 153-unit Foundry on Third and 102-unit Evergreen Landing projects, there is a need for storage options for residents with limited apartment space. No action was taken at this meeting; only the public hearing was held.

### Conditional use permit for 731 North First Street (Beacon Resources LLC)
Bornman made a motion to approve, seconded by Bugamin. With no questions or discussion from commissioners, the motion to approve the conditional use permit for the 70-unit, 7-story apartment building passed unanimously.

### Transportation project plat for Grand Avenue signal replacements at Sturgeon and Townline Road
Bugamin made a motion to approve, seconded by Balkan. With no discussion, the motion passed unanimously.

## Public Comment
One written public comment was submitted by Linda Lawrence on March 12th expressing support for the Beacon Resources apartment proposal, stating housing of this capacity will be good for downtown small businesses and citing the developer's track record. Jason Dunwy and Melinda Dunwy spoke in person during the public hearing for the storage facility at 218 South Fourth Street, advocating for the project to serve new downtown residents.

## Action Items
- Conditional use permit approved for 731 North First Street for 70-unit, 7-story apartment building (Beacon Resources LLC)
- Transportation project plat approved for Grand Avenue signal replacements at Sturgeon and Townline Road
- Vice chair election postponed to April session
