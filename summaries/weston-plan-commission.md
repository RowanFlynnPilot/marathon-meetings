## Meeting Overview
The Weston Plan Commission approved the site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection districts. The commission also approved April 2026 minutes, certified survey maps, and building permits.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review - Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Jennifer from staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. Chief Jeremy explained challenges including a 36-inch gas pipeline requiring the building to be rotated and repositioned to the north to utilize the flat area and reduce fill costs. The building will have capacity for 50-60 officers (currently 40) with expansion possible to the north. An alternate storage shed was discussed with suggestion to place it further north to allow future building expansion. Motion to approve was made and passed unanimously with conditions 2-5 as listed by staff.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection district
Jennifer presented a new inspection program requiring two annual inspections per facility - a warm weather inspection focusing on structural integrity and drainage, and a winter operational inspection focusing on material handling and containment. The program includes a standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT standards. Staff noted one existing facility near Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process and commissioners agreed staff should reach out to bring them into compliance. Motion to approve passed unanimously with addition that staff report back to both Plan Commission and Public Works Committee.

### April 13, 2026 meeting minutes
Motion to approve as presented was made and seconded. Passed unanimously without discussion.

### April 2026 certified survey maps and site plans
Staff-approved items were presented. Motion to approve passed unanimously.

### April 2026 building permits
Motion to approve passed unanimously.

### Planning and development department update
Jennifer announced the first joint Plan Commission/Village Board steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m. Staff will send email and calendar invitations.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensson of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via video conference. He urged the commission to exercise common sense regarding landscaping requirements for the new police department to avoid excessive taxpayer costs similar to the Green Heck Turner building. He also requested the commission require annual reporting on salt storage shed inspections, noting some zoning complaints are years old and get 'lost in the sauce.'
