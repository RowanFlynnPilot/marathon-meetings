## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved a site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection districts. The commission also scheduled the first steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan update for June 1st.

## Key Discussions
### Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility site plan
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. Jennifer explained the building meets all B2 zoning requirements and was rotated to address a gas pipeline easement running through the property. Chief Jeremy explained the building was designed for future growth, with current capacity for 40 officers but room for 50-60. Discussion covered the alternate storage shed placement, with commissioners suggesting it be positioned further north to allow future building expansion. The motion to approve was made and passed unanimously with conditions 2-5 as listed by staff.

### Salt storage shed inspection program - Wellhead protection district
Jennifer presented the proposed inspection program requiring two annual inspections per facility - one warm weather inspection focused on structural integrity and drainage, and one winter operational inspection. The program includes a standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT requirements. Staff noted one existing facility near Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process despite being closest to the village well. Commissioners agreed staff should reach out to that facility. The motion to approve passed unanimously, with an amendment to require reporting back to both the Plan Commission and Public Works Committee.

### April 2026 staff approved certified survey maps and site plans
Motion to approve was made, seconded, and passed unanimously without discussion.

### April 2026 building permits
Motion to approve was made, seconded, and passed unanimously without discussion.

### Planning and development department monthly project update
Jennifer announced the first steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan update is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m., with joint participation from the Plan Commission and Village Board. She committed to sending calendar invites and emails to commissioners.

### Future agenda items and residential TIF discussion
Electric vehicle charging stations were noted for a future fall agenda. A commissioner asked about potential residential TIF districts for housing development. Staff indicated Ehlers has prepared a presentation on residential TIF qualifications that could be shared with the commission, possibly as part of the comprehensive plan steering committee discussions.

## Public Comment
Jim Penson of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via online connection. He urged the commission to exercise common sense on landscaping requirements for the new police department to avoid excessive taxpayer costs similar to the Green Heck Turner building project. He also requested that the salt shed inspection program include annual reporting on site visits and findings, noting that some zoning complaints are years old.

## Action Items
- Site plan for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive approved with staff conditions 2-5
- Salt storage shed inspection program adopted for wellhead protection district with twice-annual inspections
- Staff to report inspection findings to both Plan Commission and Public Works Committee annually
- Staff to reach out to existing salt storage facility near Sternberg that lacks conditional use approval
- First comprehensive plan steering committee meeting scheduled for June 1st at 5:00 p.m.
- Staff to send calendar invites and Ehlers residential TIF presentation to commissioners
