## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved the site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection areas. Both items passed unanimously with all commissioners present voting in favor.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review - Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. The building was rotated to avoid conflicts with a 36-inch gas pipeline running through the property. Chief Jeremy explained the building will accommodate 50-60 officers for future growth beyond current staffing of 40 officers. An alternate storage shed is proposed but may be built later depending on bid costs. Discussion included future expansion possibilities to the north, landscaping compliance with current code, and drainage considerations for shed placement. Motion to approve was made and passed unanimously.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for Wellhead Protection District
Jennifer presented a new inspection program requiring two annual inspections of salt storage facilities within wellhead protection areas - one in warm weather focusing on structural integrity and one in winter focusing on operations. A standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT requirements was developed. Staff noted one existing facility near Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process and commissioners agreed staff should reach out to bring them into compliance. Michael noted the village's wells are shallow (90-110 ft) with sandy soil making contamination risk higher. Motion to approve with reporting back to both Plan Commission and Public Works passed unanimously.

### April 2026 meeting minutes
Motion to approve the April 13, 2026 meeting minutes as presented was made and passed unanimously without discussion.

### Monthly reports and permits
April 2026 staff approved certified survey maps, site plans, and building permits were approved unanimously without discussion.

### Planning department update
Jennifer announced the first joint Plan Commission and Village Board steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan update is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m. Staff will send calendar invites. Discussion included potential residential TIF districts for workforce housing, with commissioners requesting to see a presentation from Ehlers on residential TIF qualifications, possibly incorporated into the comprehensive plan process.

## Public Comment
Jim Penson of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via online participation. He urged the commission to exercise common sense regarding landscaping requirements for the police department project, citing the Green Heck Turner building as an example of excessive requirements. He also requested that the salt storage shed inspection program include annual reporting on inspection results given there are not many facilities in wellhead protection zones.
