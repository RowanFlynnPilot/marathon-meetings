## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved the site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection districts. Both items passed unanimously with all commissioners present voting in favor.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. The building was rotated to address challenges with a gas pipeline running under the parking lot and to reduce fill costs. Chief Jeremy explained the building has capacity for 50-60 officers versus current staffing of 40, with expansion possible to the north. An alternate storage shed is proposed but may be deferred based on bid costs. Commissioner Garant made the motion with conditions 2-5, which passed unanimously. Discussion included questions about future expandability and positioning of the alternate shed to accommodate future growth.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection district
Jennifer presented a new inspection program requiring two annual inspections per salt storage facility in wellhead protection areas - one warm weather inspection focusing on structural integrity and one winter operational inspection. A standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT's format will be used. Staff identified one existing facility near Sternberg that predates the wellhead protection ordinance and has never gone through the conditional use process. The commission agreed staff should reach out to bring them into compliance. Motion to approve passed unanimously with amendment to report back to both Plan Commission and Public Works committee.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensel of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston spoke via online participation. He urged the commission to exercise common sense regarding landscaping requirements for the police department project, referencing relaxed standards applied to the Green Heck Turner building. He also requested the commission require annual reporting on the salt shed inspection program to track compliance over time.
