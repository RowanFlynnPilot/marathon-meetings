## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved the site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection areas. Both items passed unanimously with the commission also scheduling a joint steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan update.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review for Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive, noting it meets all B2 zoning requirements. Chief Jeremy explained the building was rotated to address a 36-inch underground gas pipeline easement, which reduced fill costs significantly. The building has capacity for 50-60 officers (currently 40) for future growth. Discussion included potential expansion to the north, placement of an alternate storage shed, and landscaping compliance. Motion to approve was made with conditions 2-5 as listed by staff. The motion passed unanimously.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for Wellhead Protection District
Jennifer presented a new inspection program requiring two annual inspections per salt storage facility within wellhead protection areas - one warm weather inspection and one winter operational inspection. A standardized worksheet similar to DOT standards will be used. Staff noted one existing facility near Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process and should be contacted. Commission discussed the village's shallow wells (90-110 ft) and sandy soil making contamination a concern. Motion passed unanimously to approve the program with reporting back to both Plan Commission and Public Works committee.

### April 2026 meeting minutes
Motion to approve the April 13, 2026 meeting minutes as presented passed unanimously with no discussion.

### April 2026 staff approved items and building permits
Both the April 2026 staff approved certified survey maps/site plans and building permits were approved unanimously without discussion.

### Planning and development department monthly project update
Jennifer announced the first joint Plan Commission and Village Board steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m. Discussion followed about residential TIF districts and workforce housing, with mention that Ehlers has prepared a presentation on qualifications that may be shared with the commission or incorporated into comprehensive plan discussions.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensson of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via online connection. He requested the commission exercise common sense regarding landscaping requirements for the police department project, referencing previous concerns with the Greenheck Turner building. He also asked that the commission request annual reporting on salt shed inspections within wellhead protection zones, noting some zoning complaints are years old.
