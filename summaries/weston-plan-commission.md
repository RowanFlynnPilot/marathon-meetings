## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved the site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection areas. Both items passed unanimously with all commissioners present voting in favor.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. The building was rotated to address challenges with a gas pipeline easement running through the property. Chief Jeremy explained the building will accommodate 50-60 officers for future growth beyond current staffing of 40 officers. Discussion included landscaping compliance, an optional storage shed for evidence overflow, and future expansion possibilities to the north. A motion was made to recommend approval to the board of trustees subject to staff conditions 2-5. The motion passed unanimously.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection district
Staff presented a new inspection program requiring two annual inspections of salt storage facilities within wellhead protection areas - one in warm weather focusing on structural integrity and one in winter focusing on operations. The program includes a standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT requirements. Staff noted one existing facility near Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process and commissioners agreed staff should reach out to bring them into compliance. A motion to approve the program with reporting back to both the Plan Commission and Public Works Committee passed unanimously.

### April 2026 staff approved certified survey maps and site plans
Motion to approve was made and seconded. Passed unanimously without discussion.

### April 2026 building permits
Motion to approve was made and seconded. Passed unanimously without discussion.

### Planning and development department monthly project update
Staff announced the first meeting of the joint Plan Commission and Village Board steering committee for the comprehensive plan is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m. Staff will send email confirmation and calendar invites to commissioners.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensel of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via online attendance. He requested the commission use common sense regarding landscaping requirements for the police department project to avoid unnecessary taxpayer expense similar to the Green Heck Turner building. He also asked for annual reporting on the salt shed inspection program to ensure complaints don't get lost and sites are regularly monitored.
