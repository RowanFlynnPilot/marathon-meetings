## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved a site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection districts. Both items passed unanimously.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. Chief Jeremy explained the building was rotated to address challenges with a gas pipeline running through the property, which reduced fill costs significantly. The building is designed for 50-60 officer capacity for future growth. Discussion included questions about an alternate storage shed placement to allow for future expansion, landscaping compliance, and gas line construction requirements. A motion to recommend approval to the board of trustees with staff conditions 2-5 passed unanimously.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection district
Jennifer presented a proposed inspection program requiring two annual inspections per facility - a warm weather inspection focused on structural integrity and a winter operational inspection focused on material handling practices. The program includes a standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT requirements. Staff noted one existing facility near the municipal well on Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process and should be contacted. The commission approved the program with an amendment to require reporting back to both the Plan Commission and Public Works Committee. Motion passed unanimously.

### April 2026 meeting minutes
Motion to approve the April 13, 2026 meeting minutes as presented passed unanimously with no discussion.

### Planning department monthly update
Jennifer announced the first meeting of the joint Plan Commission/Village Board steering committee for the comprehensive plan is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m. She will send email confirmation and calendar invites to commissioners.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensson of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via online connection. He urged the commission to exercise common sense on landscaping requirements for the police department project to avoid excessive taxpayer costs similar to the Green Heck Turner building. He also requested that the salt shed inspection program include annual reporting on inspection results for the limited number of facilities in wellhead protection zones.

## Action Items
- Site plan for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility recommended for approval to Board of Trustees with staff conditions
- Salt storage shed inspection program approved for implementation with reporting to both Plan Commission and Public Works Committee
- Staff to reach out to existing salt storage facility on Sternberg that has not gone through wellhead protection conditional use process
- Staff to report inspection findings to Plan Commission and Public Works around May 2027
- First comprehensive plan steering committee meeting scheduled for June 1st at 5:00 p.m.
- Future agenda item: electric vehicle charging stations expected in fall
