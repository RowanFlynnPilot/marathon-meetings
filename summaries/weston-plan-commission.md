## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved site plans for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection areas. The commission also scheduled the first steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan update for June 1st.

## Key Discussions
### Site plan review for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility
Staff presented the site plan for the new police department and municipal court at 3600 Community Center Drive. Chief Jeremy explained the building orientation was changed to face east due to a gas pipeline easement, which reduced fill costs. The building will accommodate 50-60 officers for future growth beyond the current 40 officers. Discussion included an alternate storage shed location, landscaping compliance, and the gas pipeline under the parking lot (approved by ANR/gas company). A motion was made to recommend approval to the board of trustees subject to staff conditions 2-5. The motion passed unanimously.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection district
Staff presented a proposed inspection program with two annual inspections per facility - a warm weather inspection for structural integrity and a winter operational inspection for material handling practices. The program includes a standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT standards. Staff noted one existing facility near Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process and should be contacted. The commission approved the program with an amendment to report back to both the Plan Commission and Public Works Committee. Motion passed unanimously.

### April 2026 staff approved certified survey maps and site plans
The commission approved the staff report without discussion. Motion passed unanimously.

### April 2026 building permits
The commission approved the building permit report without discussion. Motion passed unanimously.

### Planning and development department monthly update
Staff announced the first steering committee meeting for the comprehensive plan update is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m., with a joint session of the Plan Commission and Village Board. Staff will send calendar invites.

### Future agenda items
Electric vehicle charging stations were noted as a future agenda item, likely coming in the fall. A commissioner asked about potential TIF districts for residential/workforce housing. Staff mentioned Ehlers has put together a presentation on residential TIF qualifications that could be shared with the commission or incorporated into the comprehensive plan steering committee discussions.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensel of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via video call. He asked the commission to exercise common sense regarding landscaping requirements for the police department project to avoid excessive taxpayer costs similar to the Green Heck Turner building. He also requested that the salt shed inspection program include annual reporting on site visits and findings.
