## Meeting Overview
The Village of Weston Plan Commission approved a site plan for the new Mountain Bay Metropolitan Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive, and adopted a new salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection areas. Both items passed unanimously with staff conditions.

## Key Discussions
### Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility site plan
Staff presented the site plan for the new police and court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive. The building was rotated to accommodate a gas pipeline easement, with parking lot now positioned over the 36-inch underground pipeline. Chief Jeremy explained the building will accommodate 50-60 officers (currently 40) for future growth. Discussion included the alternate storage shed placement for future expansion potential, with suggestion to move it further north. The landscaping plan meets current relaxed code requirements. Motion to approve with staff conditions 2-5 passed unanimously.

### Salt storage shed inspection program for wellhead protection district
Staff proposed a two-inspection-per-year program for salt storage facilities in wellhead protection areas - one warm weather inspection focusing on structural integrity and one winter operational inspection. The program includes a standardized inspection worksheet similar to DOT standards. Staff noted one existing facility near the municipal well on Sternberg has never gone through the wellhead protection conditional use process. Commission directed staff to reach out to that facility about compliance. Motion to approve the program with reporting back to both Plan Commission and Public Works passed unanimously.

### April 2026 staff approved items and building permits
Both the April 2026 staff approved certified survey maps/site plans and building permits were approved unanimously with no discussion.

### Planning and development department update
Staff announced the first meeting of the joint Plan Commission/Village Board steering committee for the comprehensive plan is scheduled for Monday, June 1st at 5:00 p.m. Staff will send calendar invites. Discussion arose about potential residential TIF districts for workforce housing, with Ehlers having provided a presentation on qualifications that may be shared with the commission or incorporated into comprehensive plan discussions.

## Public Comment
Jim Pensel of 5002 Arrow Street, Weston, spoke via remote connection. He urged the commission to exercise common sense on landscaping requirements for the police department project to avoid excessive taxpayer costs, referencing relaxed standards used for the Greenheck Turner building. He also requested that staff provide annual reports on salt shed inspections rather than letting oversight get 'lost in the sauce' like some other zoning complaints.

## Action Items
- Site plan for Mountain Bay Metro Police Department and Municipal Court facility at 3600 Community Center Drive approved with staff conditions 2-5
- Salt storage shed inspection program adopted for wellhead protection district with twice-annual inspections
- Staff to report inspection findings to both Plan Commission and Public Works committee next year
- Staff to reach out to existing salt storage facility on Sternberg about wellhead protection conditional use compliance
- Joint Plan Commission/Village Board comprehensive plan steering committee meeting scheduled for June 1st at 5:00 p.m.
- Staff to share Ehlers presentation on residential TIF district qualifications with commission
