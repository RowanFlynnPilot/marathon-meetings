## Meeting Overview
The Wausau School Board held its regular May 2026 meeting, approving several key items including the 2026-27 budget reconciliation plan, a 2.63% wage adjustment for non-teacher employee groups, teacher contract renewals, and a 5-year charter contract renewal for the Wausau Area Montessori Charter School. The meeting also featured a student presentation from Maine Elementary fifth graders and concluded with a unanimous vote to move into closed session for nonrenewal and contract evaluation matters.

## Key Discussions
### Maine Elementary Student Presentation
Principal Laura Dred introduced four fifth graders — Jaden Dura, Clay Mishka, Adeline Rembrick, and Hudson — who presented on their favorite Maine Elementary events including dress-up days, food fundraisers, Blue and Gold Day, water day, the school forest overnight trip, and the PTO's contributions to the school. Board members responded warmly, joking about adding a dunk tank or 100-foot slide to water day festivities targeting new board members or the superintendent. One board member specifically thanked Principal Dred for her leadership.

### Consent Agenda
The consent agenda covering staff appointments, replacements, contract changes, separations, retirements, meeting minutes from April 13, 27, and 30, payment of bills, budget status, investment report, and donations from organizations including Horsman Neighbors, American Online Giving Foundation, Forest Park Neighborhood, Charles Berger, Josh Dwey, and M3 Insurance was brought to a vote. The motion passed unanimously with no dissent or abstentions noted.

### 2026-27 Budget Reconciliation Plan
The budget reconciliation plan, previously presented by Assistant Superintendent of Operations Elizabeth Channel at the April Committee of the Whole, was brought for board approval. The motion was made and seconded, and the plan was approved with one abstention noted by the board president. No substantive debate was recorded.

### Teacher Contract Approvals for 2026-27
Tabitha Gundram presented the list of teacher contracts for renewal for the 2026-27 school year, noting that state statute requires contracts to be issued by the 15th. The motion passed with one abstention. No opposition or debate was recorded.

### 2.63% Wage Adjustment for Non-Teacher Employee Groups
Administration recommended a 2.63% wage adjustment for all employee groups except teachers, whose adjustment will be addressed separately through the negotiation process. Elizabeth Channel noted the item was called out for separate board action even though it was included in the budget reconciliation plan already approved. The motion passed; no vote count beyond 'motion carries' was stated, and no opposition was noted.

### Wausau Area Montessori Charter School 5-Year Contract Renewal
Elizabeth Channel presented the WAMCS 5-year charter contract renewal (2026-2031), and Montessori Governance Council President Tyler Berg spoke in person, thanking the board for 20 years of support and expressing hope for another 20 years of partnership. The motion to approve the renewal passed; the board president noted the motion carries with no recorded opposition or abstentions.

### Motion to Enter Closed Session
Superintendent Bushman requested a motion to enter closed session under Wisconsin statutes 19.85(1)(c) for final notice of nonrenewal and 19.85(1)(e) and (g) for contract evaluation. A roll call vote was taken and all eight members present — Sarah, Charles, John, Pat, Corey, Lance, Yao, and Jim — voted yes. The motion passed 8-0.

## Public Comment
One public commenter spoke: Dana (last name not given), who identified herself as a WEA member. She reported that teacher appreciation week concluded successfully with staff feeling appreciated by administration and parents, though she noted middle school students were less engaged given the time of year. She also announced a retirement celebration for retiring WEA members scheduled for June 3rd at Bull Falls Brewery.
