## Meeting Overview
The Wausau Room Tax Commission approved six tourism grants totaling over $95,000 for local events and projects, including $20,000 each for Taste N' Glow Balloon Fest, Hmong Wausau Festival, and Big Bull Falls Blues Fest, plus $25,000 for Sylvan Hill Bike Trail Maintenance. The City of Wausau's request for portable security bollards was approved at 50% of the requested amount.

## Key Discussions
### Update from the Wausau/Central Wisconsin Convention & Visitors Bureau
The Convention & Visitors Bureau provided an update to the commission. No vote was required for this informational item.

### Consideration of the minutes of the February 9, 2026 Meeting
Approved 4-0. Motion moved by Lindsey Lewitzke and seconded by Tim VanDeYacht. All members present voted yes: Michael Martens, Tim VanDeYacht, Lindsey Lewitzke, and Tom Neal.

### Tourism Grant request from Taste N' Glow Balloon Fest
Approved for $20,000. The grant will support the Taste N Glow Fest event. Detailed vote count not recorded.

### Tourism Grant request from Hmong American Center for Hmong Wausau Festival
Approved for $20,000. The grant will support the Hmong Wausau Festival. Detailed vote count not recorded.

### Tourism Grant request from EAA Chapter 640 for AirVenture Cup Race
Approved for $10,000. The grant will support the AirVenture Cup Race. Detailed vote count not recorded.

### Tourism Grant request from Wausau Events for Big Bull Falls Blues Fest
Approved for $20,000. The grant will support the Big Bull Falls Blues Fest. Detailed vote count not recorded.

### Tourism Grant request from CWOCC for Sylvan Hill Bike Trail Maintenance
Approved for $25,000. The grant will fund maintenance of the Sylvan Hill Bike Trail. Detailed vote count not recorded.

### Tourism Grant request from City of Wausau for portable security bollards
Approved at 50% of the requested monetary amount. The commission reduced the grant to half of what was originally requested. Detailed vote count not recorded.

### 2026 Financial Projection
The 2026 financial projection was on the agenda. No vote action was recorded for this item.
