## Key Discussions
### Proposed amendments to Chapter 50 Nuisances
The committee was scheduled to continue discussion on proposed amendments to the Village's nuisance ordinance. This was listed as unfinished business, indicating prior committee review of these changes.

### Proposed E-Bicycle Ordinance and Chapter 82 amendments
The committee was set to review a proposed ordinance addressing e-bicycles and related amendments to the Traffic and Vehicles chapter regarding bicycles. This was carried over as unfinished business from previous meetings.

### Overnight Parking Regulations
The committee was expected to consider changes to Section 82.107 regarding overnight parking restrictions during the October 31 to May 1 winter period. This follows public comment received at the March meeting questioning the enforceability of current parking regulations.

### Alderson Street Crosswalk Request
The committee was scheduled to discuss a request for an additional crosswalk on Alderson Street between Mount Olive Church and D.C. Everest school. This pedestrian safety improvement would affect students and churchgoers in the area.

### Mobile Food Vendors License Amendment
The committee was set to review proposed amendments to the mobile food vendors license section relating to fire code compliance and Fire Department regulations. This would update requirements for food vendor operations in the Village.

### E911 Addressing Standards Compliance
The committee was expected to consider address corrections to ensure properties comply with Village and Marathon County E911 addressing standards. This affects emergency response capabilities throughout the Village.

### 4104 Shorey Ave Raze Order Request
The committee was scheduled to discuss a raze order for the property at 4104 Shorey Avenue. As noted in the March 2026 meeting minutes, this abandoned property's owner lacks financing to repair or remove the home.
